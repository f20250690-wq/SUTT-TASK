Features

Everything you need to schedule smarter

From one-on-ones to enterprise teams, Cal.com adapts to how you work.

Smart scheduling

Share your availability and let guests pick a time that works. Automatically syncs with your calendar.

Round-robin routing

Distribute meetings evenly across your team. Set rules based on skills, workload, or priority.

150+ integrations

Connect with Zoom, Google Meet, Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce and dozens of your favorite tools.

Fully customizable

White-label your booking page. Match your brand with custom domains, colors, and layouts.

Privacy first

Open source and self-hostable. Your data stays yours. SOC 2 compliant for enterprise teams.

Analytics & insights

Track no-shows, booking trends, and team utilization. Make data-driven decisions about your time.
